References

The Nintendo Virtual Boy has been referenced in other Nintendo games such as Super Smash Bros, Melee and Warioware Inc. There also appeared one in Super Paper Mario, as a part of Francis's collection of video games.

Community

A community called Planet Virtual Boy Has been keeping this ancient tech alive producing news and guides for repairs and updates. This community has more then 1000 members and is still active to this day!

VR Attempt No.2

The second attempt by the gaming giant Nintendo to bring virtual reality to the market was far more successful, launching on March 27, 2011 the Nintendo 3DS. To this day it has sold over 75 Million units!
